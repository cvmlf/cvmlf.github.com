Points are calculated as follows: (# of competitors / 2.718281828) + 4 minus your place finished in the tournament plus (1.5 for 1st, 1.0 for 2nd, 0.5 for third) + 1.0 for big bass. You are guaranteed at least 1.0 point for showing up to a tournament and 2.0 points if you catch at least one scorable fish.
Each competitor will drop their lowest scoring tournament of the year.
