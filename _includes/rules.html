CVMLF Championship

  • The last tournament of the year is the CVMLF Championship. Club members need to qualify for this event in one of the following ways:
    1. The top finisher (who has not already qualified) from each of the five tournaments
    2. The top three anglers (who have not already qualified) in the point standings after five tournaments.
    3. Any angler who has fished in all five regular season tournaments will automatically qualify.
  • The championship is different than a regular season tournament in the following ways:
    1. Period 1 is an elimination round where half of the anglers will be eliminated
    2. The elimination round will have a target weight, where once an angler reaches the target weight they automatically advance to the championship round.
    3. Period 2 is the championship round and begins with all weights set to zero
  • Awards will be given out to the top three finishers in the championship as well as the big bass of the tournament.



Special Awards

  • Angler Of The Year - awarded to the angler who leads the points standings at the end of the five regular season tournaments
  • Big Bass Of The Year - awarded to the angler who catches the single biggest bass of the five regular season tournaments
